Our Story

Bringing Thai Flavours to Warwickshire

Nur Nur Paradise opened in November 2023, bringing authentic Thai fusion cuisine to Kenilworth. We blend traditional Thai flavors with Western influences in our beautifully renovated Warwick Road restaurant, founded by Lee Wongsalees and Chantana Nimcean. Originally from Thailand, we chose Kenilworth for its vibrant community spirit and welcoming atmosphere. Our restaurant's name, "Nur Nur," translates as "delicious" in Thai—a promise we fulfill through every carefully crafted dish we serve.
