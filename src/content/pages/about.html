About Us

Behind The Flavours

Nur Nur Paradise opened its doors on November 10, 2023, bringing a unique Thai fusion dining experience to Kenilworth. Our menu artfully blends traditional Thai cuisine with Western influences, creating distinctive dishes that honor both culinary traditions.

Founded by Lee Wongsalees and Chantana Nimcean, our Warwick Road restaurant represents the realisation of a shared dream. After meeting in 2014 while working at a high street chain in Marlow, Buckinghamshire, we combined our extensive hospitality experience and passion for authentic Thai flavors to create something truly special.

The journey to Nur Nur Paradise began in January 2023 when we first discussed opening our own restaurant. After exploring various locations across the region, we fell in love with Kenilworth's vibrant community atmosphere and thriving high street—the perfect setting for our culinary vision.

As natives of Thailand, we've created a warm, welcoming space where guests feel at home while enjoying exceptional fusion cuisine unavailable anywhere else in Kenilworth.

Our name 'Nur Nur' translates to 'delicious' in Thai—it's not just what we're called, it's what we promise with every dish we serve. Lee Wongsalees Owner
