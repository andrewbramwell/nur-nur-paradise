Absolutely delicious food - definitely recommend the starter platter, tamarind duck, lamb shank massaman curry, honey roasted duck and the weeping tiger! - but everything that came out of the kitchen looked equally delightful. This is accompanied by amazing service from very friendly and attentive staff, a reasonably priced wine list and a lovely relaxed atmosphere. We'll definitely be back!

Sarah Moreton

First time lunch here with a friend and it was one of the best meals I have had in a long time, clean and well presented inside, service was friendly and spot on, the food was absolutely delicious, I had the zap zap pinto lunch platter which was plentiful and treated myself to a dessert- mango and sticky rice, was heaven! My new favourite restaurant thank you!
